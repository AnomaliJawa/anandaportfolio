About Me.

I'm a Computer Science student with a strong interest in UI/UX design, tech education, and community development. I’ve held leadership roles such as Co-Leader and Administrative Manager at GDG OC BINUS Malang, where I co-managed a 700+ member tech community, led strategic events, and built collaborations with industry partners and international student groups.

As a UI/UX Educator at Bina Nusantara Computer Club, I developed and delivered design learning modules across multiple campuses, mentoring participants in building their first prototypes and applying their skills to real-world projects.

I also serve as a Freshmen Partner and Leader, guiding first-year students through mentoring and community service activities. My background includes digital content creation, public speaking, and data-driven outreach with a focus on building inclusive, engaging, and impactful technology programs.
